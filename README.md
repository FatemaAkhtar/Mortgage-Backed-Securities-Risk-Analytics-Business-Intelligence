# 🏦 Mortgage-Backed Securities Risk Analytics

## Transforming 53.8 Million Mortgage Records into Executive Risk Intelligence

---

## Executive Summary

Mortgage-backed securities portfolios generate millions of loan-level transactions that influence lending strategies, investment decisions, and financial risk management.

As portfolio size grows, identifying delinquency trends, borrower risk, and portfolio exposure through traditional reporting becomes increasingly complex and time-consuming.

This project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can convert large-scale mortgage data into a centralized executive reporting solution that enables portfolio managers, risk analysts, and business stakeholders to monitor credit quality, borrower behavior, and lending performance through interactive analytics.

---

# Business Problem

Risk teams needed a scalable analytical solution capable of answering questions that directly influence lending decisions.

Without centralized reporting:

* High-risk borrower segments were difficult to identify
* Portfolio exposure required manual analysis across multiple reports
* Delinquency trends lacked executive visibility
* Borrower behavior could not be easily compared across credit profiles, loan purposes, and regions

The challenge was not simply to visualize data, but to transform millions of mortgage records into meaningful business intelligence that supports faster and more informed decision making.

---

# Analytical Approach

The solution followed an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow.

### Business Understanding

Studied mortgage portfolio management, borrower risk indicators, delinquency metrics, and lending performance to establish a strong analytical foundation.

### SQL Data Preparation

The raw mortgage dataset was transformed into an analytical model by:

* Validating loan records
* Standardizing borrower attributes
* Creating Credit Score, LTV and DTI segments
* Building business-friendly risk categories
* Optimizing data structures for Power BI reporting

### Business Intelligence

Developed executive dashboards focused on portfolio monitoring, borrower intelligence, and credit performance, enabling stakeholders to explore risk factors through interactive visual analytics.

---

# Solution Architecture

The complete analytical solution consists of six interconnected executive dashboards covering:

* Portfolio Risk & Exposure
* Credit Performance & Delinquency
* Borrower & Lending Intelligence
* Loan Recovery & Loss Severity
* Borrower Behavioral Insights
* Risk Segmentation & Delinquency Modeling

This repository showcases three representative dashboards highlighting the overall analytical framework and reporting strategy.

---

# Business Impact

The solution transforms **53.8+ million mortgage records** into an executive decision-support platform that enables stakeholders to:

✔ Monitor portfolio health through centralized KPIs

✔ Identify high-risk borrower segments before portfolio deterioration

✔ Analyze delinquency and credit performance trends across multiple dimensions

✔ Compare lending behavior by borrower profile, property type, and geography

✔ Reduce manual reporting effort through interactive self-service analytics

✔ Support data-driven portfolio management and risk mitigation strategies

Instead of reviewing millions of individual loan records, business users can interactively explore portfolio performance through a unified reporting experience.

---

# Key Analytical Insights

* Portfolio risk can be evaluated through borrower credit quality, LTV, DTI, and delinquency indicators rather than isolated loan metrics.

* Interactive segmentation enables faster identification of borrower groups requiring additional monitoring.

* Combining borrower demographics, lending characteristics, and repayment behavior provides a more comprehensive view of mortgage portfolio performance.

* Executive dashboards improve visibility into portfolio exposure and support strategic lending decisions through centralized reporting.
