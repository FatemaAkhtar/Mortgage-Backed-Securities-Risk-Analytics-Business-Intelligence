Financial institutions manage millions of mortgage loans where borrower behavior, credit quality, delinquency, and repayment patterns directly influence portfolio performance and lending decisions.

# 🎯 Business Challenge

Mortgage portfolios generate enormous volumes of transactional data containing borrower profiles, repayment history, credit information, and loan characteristics.

Without centralized analytics:

* Portfolio exposure is difficult to monitor
* High-risk borrower segments remain hidden within millions of records
* Delinquency analysis requires manual reporting
* Credit performance lacks executive visibility
* Business teams spend more time preparing reports than generating insights

The objective was to transform complex mortgage data into an executive Business Intelligence solution capable of supporting portfolio managers, credit analysts, and business stakeholders with faster, data-driven decisions.

---

# 💼 Solution Approach

The project followed a complete Business Intelligence workflow from business understanding to executive reporting.

### Business Understanding

Studied mortgage portfolio management, borrower risk indicators, lending performance, delinquency metrics, and credit quality to establish business requirements.

### SQL Data Preparation

The raw mortgage dataset was transformed into a business-ready analytical model by:

* Data validation and quality checks
* Standardizing borrower attributes
* Creating Credit Score, LTV, and DTI segments
* Building borrower risk categories
* Implementing business rules
* Optimizing reporting tables for Power BI

### Business Intelligence

Developed interactive executive dashboards focused on:

* Portfolio Risk & Exposure
* Credit Performance & Delinquency
* Borrower & Lending Intelligence

allowing stakeholders to explore portfolio performance through dynamic KPI-driven analytics.
