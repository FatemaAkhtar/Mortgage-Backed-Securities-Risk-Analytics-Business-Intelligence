\# Business Understanding



\# Mortgage-Backed Securities Risk Analytics



\## Business Context



Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS) are investment products created by pooling thousands of mortgage loans into tradable financial assets. Financial institutions and investors rely on continuous portfolio monitoring to evaluate borrower performance, identify emerging risks, and make informed lending and investment decisions.



Given the scale of mortgage portfolios, manual analysis becomes inefficient and often delays critical business decisions.



\---



\# Business Challenge



The organization needed a centralized analytical solution capable of transforming millions of mortgage records into meaningful business intelligence.



The primary objective was not simply to visualize data, but to enable stakeholders to quickly understand portfolio performance, borrower behavior, and credit risk through interactive reporting.



Key business goals included:



\* Improve visibility into mortgage portfolio health

\* Detect high-risk borrower segments

\* Monitor delinquency and foreclosure trends

\* Analyze borrower demographics and lending patterns

\* Support proactive portfolio and risk management decisions



\---



\# Industry Understanding



Mortgage portfolios are influenced by multiple financial indicators including:



\* Credit Score

\* Loan-to-Value (LTV)

\* Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI)

\* Interest Rate

\* Loan Purpose

\* Occupancy Status

\* Property Type

\* Delinquency History



These variables collectively determine borrower risk and long-term portfolio performance.



Understanding the relationship between these metrics is essential for effective mortgage risk management.



\---



\# Analytical Approach



The analytical workflow followed four major stages:



\### 1. Business Understanding



Studied mortgage lending concepts, portfolio management principles, delinquency indicators, and financial risk metrics to understand the business domain.



\### 2. Data Preparation



Used SQL to validate, standardize, and transform mortgage loan data into business-friendly analytical structures.



Transformation included:



\* Credit score segmentation

\* LTV categorization

\* DTI grouping

\* Risk classification

\* Data quality validation

\* Business rule implementation



\### 3. Business Intelligence



Designed executive dashboards focused on portfolio monitoring, credit performance, and borrower intelligence.



\### 4. Insight Generation



Translated analytical findings into business recommendations that support lending strategy and portfolio management.



\---



\# Business Questions Addressed



The analytical solution was designed to help stakeholders answer questions such as:



\* Which borrower segments contribute the highest portfolio risk?

\* How do credit score and LTV influence delinquency?

\* Which regions require additional monitoring?

\* Are first-time homebuyers associated with higher default rates?

\* Which loan purposes contribute the largest portfolio exposure?

\* How does borrower behavior impact long-term mortgage performance?

The complete reporting solution consists of six executive dashboards designed for different analytical perspectives.



This portfolio showcases three representative dashboards covering:



\* Portfolio Risk \& Exposure

Together, these dashboards provide a comprehensive view of mortgage portfolio performance through executive KPIs and interactive analytics.

\# Business Outcome



The solution transforms more than 53 million mortgage records into a centralized Business Intelligence platform that enables:



\* Executive portfolio monitoring

\* Borrower risk segmentation

\* Credit performance analysis

\* Lending behavior exploration

\* Self-service reporting

\* Data-driven decision making



Instead of reviewing millions of transactional records, stakeholders can quickly identify trends, monitor portfolio health, and prioritize risk mitigation strategies through interactive dashboards.
